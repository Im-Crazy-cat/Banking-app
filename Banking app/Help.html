What other peoples ask??

  • info_outlineHow can I arrange replacement debit card to be sent to me??

    If you need to get a replacement card, please send us a secure E-message via online banking where we can assist you with a replacement. Alternatively you can go to your local branch to order a replacement card

  • info_outlineHow do I change my address??

    You can easily update your home address online or by visiting us in branch. Please see our Change of Address support page for further information.

  • info_outlineHow do I order a new cheque book?

    If you're registered for Internet Banking you can order a new cheque book by logging on and sending us a Secure E-message. Otherwise, if you're not registered for Internet Banking you can order a new cheque book at your nearest branch. Please ensure you bring your ID with you..

  • info_outlineWhere can I find my IBAN?

    If you're registered for Internet Banking, you can log in, find the required card, and select 'Card Activation'. Or if you're activating a replacement card and know the PIN you can also do this from any HSBC ATM in the UK.

  • info_outlineHow do I activate Credit card?

    If you're registered for Internet Banking, you can log in, find the required card, and select 'Card Activation'. Or if you're activating a replacement card and know the PIN you can also do this from any HSBC ATM in the UK.
